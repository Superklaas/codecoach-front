CodeCoach

CodeCoach is a platform that allows you to get coached on coding issues.

Developers with difficulties when coding can ask for explanations and advice from more experienced developers in their organisation.

With CodeCoach everything is quick and easy: just register as a Coach or request a coaching session on this website, and it will organize a private coaching session in the organisation.

Become a Coach

To help others and increase your potential

If you feel strong in a laguage or framework and want to help other developers, sign up as a coach at your organisation, you will strengthen you knowledge in this subject, you will develop new skills and abova all you will feel useful. Your commitment will be recognized and rewarded by your organisation.

Benefit from coaching

To improve your code results and regain motivation

If you have difficulties when coding and you want to progress, book an appointment for private coaching, and benefit from the developing and practical experience from another developer at your organisation, who understands your concrete needs. Your willingness to progress will be recognized by your organisation.
